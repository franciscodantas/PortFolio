About Me

FullStack Developer

Hello! My name is Francisco Antonio Dantas de Sousa and I'm a technology professional with a wide range of skills. Throughout my career, I have participated in several national and regional robotics competitions as part of the Robossauros team from Sousa, where I was a champion in the FTC category in 2020. I have a fast learning ability and a strong aptitude for logic and mathematics. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from UFCG. Combining my technical skills with my academic background, I am committed to continuously developing myself and achieving success in the technology industry. I am open to opportunities that allow me to apply my skills and knowledge in technology, safety, and computing, as well as working as part of a team to achieve common goals. If you'd like to connect with me, please don't hesitate to get in touch. I'm always looking for new opportunities and partnerships.
